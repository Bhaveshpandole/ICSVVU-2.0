Profile of the Institute




Institute of Computer Science with its premier objective to impart quality education in the area of computer science, continuously meeting its objective since 1989. Keeping the goal to deliver the latest and relevant knowledge with the practical aspects, institute has successfully completed more than 24 years of establishment.



Institute is offering Ph.D., M.Phil., M.C.A., M.Sc. (Computer Science, Information Technology, CS with Data Science, CS with AI & Machine Learning, CS with Information Security), BCA, BCA (Hons.), BSc-CS(Hons.), 4 PGDCSA, 11 PG Diploma, 13 UG Diploma and 26 Certificate courses (Total 65 Programmes).



This Institute is a University teaching department and one of the premiere Institute of Central India Region. Our students are prepared in the most modern teaching and learning environment, created as per latest need of IT sector and are well placed in a reputed industrial houses across the word. Students of this Institute are placed in almost all of the top organizations of country and world like: Oracle, IBM, InfoSys, Wipro, HCL, TCS, Tata Infotech, Accenture, PCS, MBT, Mahindra, Polaris, GE, CSC, Impetus, Ranbaxy, Eiljy-Lily, IDBI Bank, ICICI group, Birla Sun Life, Asian Paints, Bajaj Aliang, Genpact, Caprus-KPO, ZEE-Group and many more….04 Research Scholars of this Institute received Rajiv Gandhi Junior Research Fellowship award by University Grant Commission UGC New Delhi during 2011-2013.


Founded in 1956, Vikram University is a non-profit public higher-education institution located in the urban setting of the medium city of Ujjain (population range of 250,000-499,999 inhabitants),
Madhya Pradesh. Officially recognized by the University Grants Commission of India, Vikram University (VU) is a large (uniRank enrollment range: 10,000-14,999 students)
coeducational Indian higher education institution. Vikram University (VU) offers courses and programs leading to officially recognized higher education degrees
in several areas of study. See the uniRank degree levels and areas of study matrix below for further details. VU also provides several academic and
non-academic facilities and services to students including a library, as well as administrative services.
